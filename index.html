Have a Regal Experience at Clarks Avadh - Five-Star Hotel in Lucknow

As Lucknow's first and oldest five-star hotel, Clarks Avadh is a heritage landmark blending elegant architecture with modern luxury. Its prime location along the Gomti River offers picturesque views and easy access to key city landmarks. Our luxury hotel in Lucknow is situated on Mahatma Gandhi Marg and is surrounded by historic and cultural sites like Bara Imambara and Chattar Manzil. Its central position also provides convenient access to Hazratganj, the High Court, and both the Lucknow Airport and Railway Station, ensuring a comfortable and accessible stay for all travellers.

For those celebrating life’s most special moments, our elegant architecture and beautiful indoor and outdoor event spaces provide the perfect backdrop. We offer personalised services to create truly memorable wedding experiences, ensuring your celebration is as unique as your love story. Whether you're here for sightseeing, business, or a celebration, our hotel provides an accessible and comfortable stay, embodying the true spirit of a five-star hotel in Lucknow.

Luxurious Rooms

This five star hotel in Lucknow, located in Hazratganj, provides all its guests with uber-luxurious accommodation and a variety of innovative features. Being one of the best hotels in the city, it offers 6 indoor event halls that are equipped with instant facilities like photocopier, personal e-mail, fax, phone facility, Wi-Fi, audio and visual equipment, and many more, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travellers. Recognised as a premier luxury hotel in Lucknow, it stands out as one of the best 5-star hotels for corporate clients, offering world-class comfort, convenience, and unmatched hospitality.
